About All Fitness

HITT Classes

HITT is our high intensity work out group training sessions from beginner to advance atheletes who are wanting to shed some pounds or just keep in shape all year around.

Muscle Group

Muscle Group training is a focus on build lean Muscles and strength for those who want to build muscle and gain alot of strength.

Crossfit Training

Crossfit Training has grown in popularity over the past few years, its a mixture of speed, strength and explosive power this course is a supervised course with one of our fitness coaches and scheduled for certain times during the week.

Cycling Classes

Cycling is done in our studio, based on long marathon cycling events from around the world, this course is fun and will leave you soaked in your own salt, our bikes are based around cycling courses around the world!
